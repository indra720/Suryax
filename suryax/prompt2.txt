step-1 ======


Create a high-end, ultra-modern real estate website homepage for Jaipur real estate (buy properties). The website name is "PinkCity Homes" or "JaipurNest Luxury Homes". It should look premium, luxurious, and visually stunning like 2026 luxury real estate portals.

Overall Design Style:

- Quiet luxury aesthetic with clean minimalism + Rajasthan royal touch

- Color palette: Deep teal (#00332E) and saffron orange (#FF9933) as accents, soft gold (#D4AF37), pure white background, dark gray text. Support light & dark mode.

- Typography: Bold modern sans-serif headings (Poppins or Inter), elegant body text.

- High-quality cinematic hero images with Jaipur vibes (modern high-rise apartments + Amer Fort silhouette + Aravalli hills in background).

- Smooth animations, hover effects (scale + soft shadow + quick info overlay), parallax scrolling on sections.

- Fully responsive, mobile-first, fast-loading feel.

Layout Structure:

1. Sticky Navbar (Top)

   - Logo left: "PinkCity Homes" with subtle Jaipur palace icon

   - Menu: Buy | Rent | New Projects | Localities | Builders | Insights

   - Right side: Location selector "Jaipur" (dropdown with areas), "Post Property" (prominent orange button), "Login / Sign Up"

2. Hero Section (Full-screen, most impactful)

   - Cinematic background image: Luxurious modern apartment balcony overlooking Jaipur city skyline at golden hour with pink fort in distance.

   - Big bold headline in white with slight shadow: "Discover Your Royal Home in Pink City"

   - Subheadline: "2,800+ Verified Properties • Ready to Move & New Launches in Vaishali Nagar, Mansarovar, Malviya Nagar & More"

   - Large centered search bar with tabs: Buy | Rent | Projects | Plots

   - Inside search: 

     - Input: "Search by Project or Locality (Vaishali Nagar, Mansarovar, Jagatpura...)"

     - Property Type dropdown: Apartment, Villa, Independent House, Plot

     - Budget range slider: ₹20 Lakhs - ₹5 Crores+

     - BHK: 1, 2, 3, 4+

   - Prominent CTA button: "Search Properties" (glowing orange)

   - Below: Quick filters chips – "New Launches 2026" | "Luxury Villas" | "Affordable Homes" | "Ready to Move"

3. Why Jaipur 2026 Section

   - 4 elegant cards with icons: High ROI • World-class Infrastructure • Green & Modern Living • Royal Legacy Meets Future

4. Featured & Hot Properties Section

   - Heading: "Handpicked Properties in Jaipur"

   - Responsive masonry/grid of 6-8 premium property cards

   - Each card:

     - Large hero image with overlay tags ("Ready to Move" green, "New Launch" blue, "Premium" gold)

     - Project name (e.g., Ashiana Rangoli Gardens, Mahima Florenza, Vinayak Royal Crysta)

     - Location: Vaishali Nagar / Mansarovar Extension / Malviya Nagar

     - Price: ₹85 Lakhs - ₹1.8 Cr | 3 BHK | 1850 sq.ft.

     - Small amenities icons (Clubhouse, Swimming Pool, Gym, Parking, 24x7 Security)

     - Hover effect: Scale up + "View Details • Get Brochure • Schedule Visit"

     - Price trend: "↑ 14% in last year"

5. Popular Localities Section

   - Heading: "Explore Top Localities in Jaipur"

   - Grid or horizontal scroll cards for: Vaishali Nagar (Premium), Mansarovar (Family Favorite), Malviya Nagar (Central), Jagatpura, Ajmer Road, C-Scheme (Luxury)

   - Each card: Locality name, Avg price/sqft, Number of projects, "Explore" button + small map pin

6. Top Builders & New Launches

   - Carousel or grid of builder logos (Mahima, Ashiana, Manglam, Vatika, Omaxe etc.)

   - Separate section: "New Launches & Upcoming Projects 2026" with similar property cards

7. Market Insights Teaser

   - Small stats: Average price ₹5,800/sqft • High demand in Vaishali Nagar Extension

   - "Jaipur Real Estate Report 2026" card with "Read Now" button

Footer:

- Comprehensive links, city areas, property types, contact, social icons

- Tagline: "Live Royal in Modern Jaipur"

Additional Details:

- Use lots of high-resolution real estate photography with soft natural lighting.

- Make property cards look expensive and clickable.

- Add subtle Rajasthan elements (jaali patterns, elephant motifs very lightly) without making it traditional.

- Overall vibe: Sophisticated, trustworthy, aspirational, conversion-focused (lots of CTAs like "Book Site Visit", "Download Brochure", "Instant Callback").

Generate this as a complete modern website UI/UX design in high detail, cinematic style, 4K resolution, perfect for frontend development reference.
